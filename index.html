My Interests

Cognitive Learning

Learning is an integral part of our lives. Finding ways to organize, optimizing and make learing more efficient would save us much time and icrease our ability to tap into the knowledge we have to solve problems.

That is why cognitive learning (The science of learning) is among one of my interests. I find research in this field very interesting for example from Barbara Oakely: Learn how to Learn and Yana Yeinstein: Teaching the science of learning.

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics are definately fascinating endevors. Having a midset devloped from the interaction with STEM goes a long way in creatively solvng problems.

The ability to integrate scientific principles from fields like physics, math, biology with technology is just mind blowing. I draw some of my ispiration in engineering from countries such as Singapore, Israel and Germany.

Hardware

Hardware architecture and design is quite intriquing for me, I believe a fundamental understanding of how hardware works is the beginning of creating meaningful solutions.

Understanding basic electronics, computer hardware architectures, and how hardware interacts with software provides a holistic view of the world today given that there are billions of devices and the numbers keep growing.

Data communication and networks

Like Hardware, data communication and networks are core fundamental technologies. Understanding networking principles makes it possible to interconnect hardware devices.

Networking is the backbone of the internet as we know it today. With the advent of APIs (Application Programming interface) in networking, things keep getting more interesting. The ability to create user communication interfaces for programs written on top of chips and operating systems is just amazing.

Internet of Things

Getting to know hardware and networking fundamentals is just the beginning. All the fun lies in IoT, simply take that hardware knowledge and have fun.

You do this by creating hardware objects and things that interconnect with each other. IoT raises the possibilites in our world high. Did I mention you can perform complex AI and Machine learning algorithms on this tiny devices?

Cloud Technologies

Have you noticed that many of the tech interactions today happen in the cloud?

Well, I used to think the cloud meant some virtual mystical place in space where we store our data. Turns out in very low terms its just renting powerful compute power and services and access them from anywhere in the world. Connecting IoT devices, apps and intelligent edge to cloud makes me go wow!.

From math to data data-structures and algorithms, I beleive understanding the core fundamentals of Computer Science goes along way into creativity and problem solving.
